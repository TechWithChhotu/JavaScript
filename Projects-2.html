About BMI The Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of health based on weight and height. It's used to determine whether someone has a healthy or not.
BMI Guide
  • Under Weight: less than 18.6 (BMI)
  • Normal: 18.6 to 24.9 (BMI)
  • OverWeight: more than 24.9 but below 30 (BMI)
  • Obesity Class I: More Than 30 But Below 35 (BMI)
  • Obesity Class II: Over 35 But Below 40 (BMI)
